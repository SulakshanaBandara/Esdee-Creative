About Our Company

Our Story

Esdee Creative Services (Pvt) Ltd stands at the forefront of strategic innovation and sustainable growth, leveraging deep local insight and international expertise to empower our clients. We specialize in investment consulting, project management, and technical advisory, delivering tailored solutions that drive success across the hospitality, agribusiness, renewable energy, and teacher education sectors.

Our multidisciplinary team combines innovative thinking with reliable execution, ensuring strategic leadership in every project. With a steadfast commitment to excellence and a visionary approach, we inspire confidence and deliver impactful results, guiding businesses toward a resilient and prosperous future.

Our Mission

To bridge local opportunities with global standards through innovative investment solutions and expert project management, fostering sustainable growth across key sectors.

Our Vision

To be the premier partner for sustainable development in Sri Lanka, recognized for our integrity, expertise, and transformative impact on businesses and communities.

Board of Directors

Meet the leadership team guiding our strategic direction

Dr. Manouri Samarasinghe

Education Specialist

Dr. Samarasinghe is an accomplished educator and scholar in the field of English Language Teaching, with more than 20 years of international academic experience. She holds a Bachelor of Education in English from the National Institute of Education, Sri Lanka (2002), a Master's in English Language Teaching from the University of Southampton, UK (2004), and a Doctor of Education from the University of Sheffield, UK (2018).

Currently serving as a member of academic staff at Sultan Qaboos University in Oman, Dr. Samarasinghe is recognized for her contributions to curriculum development and academic research. Her expertise bridges local insights with global standards, empowering students and educators alike through transformative teaching methodologies.

Captain Chandane Samarasinghe

Maritime Expert

Captain Chandane Samarasinghe is a veteran commercial ship captain with over 20 years of command experience navigating international waters. His maritime career is marked by leadership excellence, operational precision, and an unwavering commitment to safety and compliance.

With extensive experience commanding a variety of commercial vessels, Captain Samarasinghe brings deep expertise in ship management, crew leadership, and strategic maritime operations. His reputation in the industry reflects both his technical mastery and his ability to lead effectively in high-pressure, global environments. Whether at sea or onshore, he stands as a trusted and highly respected figure in the world of commercial shipping.

Mr. Samantha Perera

Investment Strategist

Mr. Perera is a seasoned professional with over 30 years of expertise in Cost Management, Investment Strategy, and Vocational Training. He has led diverse development and investment initiatives across Sri Lanka, with a specialized focus on the Hospitality, Agribusiness, Garment Manufacturing, and Infrastructure sectors.

Throughout his career, Mr. Perera has played a pivotal role in the pre- and post-contract cost management of high-profile and VVIP projects in Oman, earning recognition for his precision and strategic acumen. In Sri Lanka, he has been managing export-oriented garment ventures, alongside championing model farm and renewable energy projects.

A strong advocate for skills development, he has contributed significantly to workforce training and apprenticeship programs in the engineering and craft sectors. With a results-driven approach and a commitment to operational excellence, Mr. Perera continues to drive forward innovative and sustainable investment models in the region.
